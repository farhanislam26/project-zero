Our Mission

The Fahrenheit Bookstore is committed to offering a quality selection of affordable new, bargain, gently read, and electronic books, as well as gifts and educational items for customers of all ages and backgrounds.

We are passionate about the written word and seek to share our love of reading to help create and sustain a community of lifelong readers.

We strive to provide a hospitable and nurturing environment to encourage the healthy exchange of ideas. We are committed to engaging with the many diverse peoples and organizations of our community. Come join us today!
